## 2. Roles, Responsibilities & Features

### 👤 System Administrator (`admin@hriq.com`)
The System Administrator maintains control over organizational access, user enrollment, and password allocation.
* **Responsibilities**:
  * Allocate accounts and assign permissions (HR vs. CEO roles).
  * Respond to forgot password requests submitted by team members.
* **Key Features**:
  * **User Management Board**: Add new team members with names, custom emails, passwords, and roles. Delete accounts instantly.
  * **Reset Assistance Queue**: Approve password changes for lockouts and track pending reset operations.

---

### 👥 HR Operations Partner (`dhanushyahr@hriq.com`)
HR operations manage candidates, jobs, sourcing pipelines, and evaluation pipelines.
* **Responsibilities**:
  * Author and publish open job descriptions.
  * Screen incoming resumes and coordinate interviews.
  * Sourcing candidates from external resources (e.g. GitHub API).
* **Key Features**:
  * **Intake Hub**: Upload resumes in bulk (PDF, DOCX) or point to local folders to ingest candidates.
  * **AI Assistant & Sourcing Engines**: Screen candidates against specific Job Descriptions using AI, search external portals like GitHub, and draft automated emails.
  * **Job Management Console**: Create, delete, and publish job positions to the public Careers Portal.
  * **Pipeline Kanban**: Move candidates through workflow stages (Applied, Screening, Interview, Offered).

---

### 📊 CEO Executive Member (`dhanushyaceo@hriq.com`)
The CEO role receives high-level executive analytics to monitor the health of organizational hiring.
* **Responsibilities**:
  * Analyze macro hiring trends and top talent matches.
  * Review role distributions and applicant fit scores.
* **Key Features**:
  * **Metrics Dashboard**: View total positions, overall applicants, and distribution of AI Fit categories (*Best Fit*, *Maybe*, *Not Fit*).
  * **Visual Graphs**: Real-time charts for application breakdown per role and candidate experience distributions.
  * **Talent Ledger**: High-level table of matching candidates, highlighting top fit match scores.

---

### 🌐 Public Candidate (Careers Portal)
The Careers Portal represents the public-facing entry point for candidates.
* **Responsibilities**:
  * Explore active job openings in the organization.
  * Upload resumes and receive instant alignment assessments.
* **Key Features**:
  * **Premium Landing Page**: Interactive "Why HRIQ" highlights and open roles.
  * **Instant AI Feedback**: Candidates get immediate match scores and fit summaries generated by the HRIQ AI Matching engine.
